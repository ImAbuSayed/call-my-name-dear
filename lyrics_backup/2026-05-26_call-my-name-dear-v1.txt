[Hook]
I see the moonlight resting on your skin
A quiet fire burning deep within
Don't need a crowd, don't need a single word
Just let my whisper be the only thing that's heard

[Verse 1]
The midnight coffee on the corner street
Our shadows dancing to an acoustic beat
I pull you closer by the open door
This is the moment I've been waiting for

Let the tension break and leave a lasting mark
